Movement Keys

  • W: Move forward
  • A: Turn left
  • S: Move backward
  • D: Turn right
  • Q: Strafe left
  • E: Strafe right
  • Space bar: Move up and zoom out
  • X: Move down and zoom in

Goal: Move all the disks from their platform to the target platform (under spotlight).

Rules

  1. Only one disk can be moved at a time.
  2. A Each move consists of taking the upper disk from one of the stacks and placing it on top of another stack or on an empty rod.
  3. No disk may be placed on top of a disk that is smaller than it.
